## 📋 Understanding the Display

The panel shows icons and text. Each part has a meaning. 

- **Color Coded Bars:** These show if the AI is active, idle, or needs your input. Green means working. Yellow means waiting for your choice. Red means a connection error.
- **Process List:** A scrollable list shows the active AI agents.
- **Permission Requests:** Blue notifications pop up when an agent wants access to a file. You can allow or deny these from the panel.

## ❓ Troubleshooting Common Issues

Use these steps if the app does not show your AI tools.

- **Restart the application:** Close the app from the system tray and open it again.
- **Check tool paths:** Make sure your coding tools are running before you start CodeIsland.
- **Update your tools:** Older versions of coding apps might not send the right status signals. Check for updates for your specific editor.
- **Check the logs:** If issues persist, go to the Help menu and select View Logs. The logs show the specific error codes causing the disconnect.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data

Your data stays on your machine. CodeIsland does not send your code or your chat history to any server. It only reads the status signals sent by your coding tools to the local system memory. No personal data leaves your device. This ensures your intellectual property remains under your control.
